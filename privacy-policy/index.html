Privacy Policy — Tide Keeper

Last updated: 18 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tide Keeper (the “Game”), published by Warebit (the “Developer”, “we”), handles your information.

Short version

The Game does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It runs entirely on your device and works fully offline.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect, request, or transmit any personal or device information. The Game has no user accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress — records, in-game “Pearls” currency, purchased upgrades, unlocked characters, daily/weekly progress, and settings — is saved only locally on your device (in the app’s private storage). It is never uploaded, shared, or transmitted anywhere. If you uninstall the Game, this local data is removed by the operating system.

Network access

The Game requests no internet permission and makes no network connections.

Advertising & analytics

The Game contains no ads and no analytics SDKs, and does not use any advertising identifier.

Third parties

We do not share any data with third parties, because we do not collect any.

Children

The Game collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the Game adds features that collect or transmit data (for example, opt-in rewarded ads or in-app purchases), we will update this policy and the Google Play “Data safety” section before releasing that version, and disclose what is collected and why.

Contact

Questions about this policy: dev@warebit.net
